Intro

What is this thing?

The app is meant to help with picking teams in our weekly pool. The idea is, once the spreads have been set, we can login and select the teams we think are going to win that week.

Moreover, we can discuss each game with each other and try to make the best decision we can each week. If you get some last minute gossip on a team, add it to the comments, and help us pick the winner.

We can also see how we're doing every week. We can get an overview of how we did each week, and we can see how the rest of us faired.

Why did you write this?

I have a weird addiction to coding. I got bored a few weekends while my picks weren't panning out, and I thought I could put it together fairly quickly. I thought we needed something more colraborative. It is by no means complete, and I do have other ideas for it, but only if you guys find it useful. If you want more, email me! If you hate it, let me know how I can make it better.

Home page

After logging in, you're greeted with this week's games. Here's a screen shot of the things you can do:
  • You can select the team you think will cover the spread. Just selecting the team will automatically save your selection.
  • You can pass on games that you're unsure of.
  • You can click any game row and enter the game view in order to discuss the game or to see what the other guys have to say about the game.
  • You can click the "League Picks" in order to get a high-level view of your picks against the rest of the guys.

Game View

On the game view page, you can see what team the other guys have picked. You can also get in on the discussion about the game in the comment section below. Know something the rest of us don't? Why not share it in the comments? Help us make the best decision.

Week Overview

In the Week overview, you can see all the picks that you made, and you can see the games that you passed on. You can also see the picks that the rest of the guys have made that week.

Previous Week Overview

If you go back to previous weeks, you can see how your picks turned out. You can also see your record for that week. Teams that you picked and covered the spread have light green backgrounds. While teams that you picked that didn't cover the spread have a light pink background.
